Subtractive optimally localized averaging (SOLA) and discrete linear inverse (DLI) methods: a unified framework for seismic tomography

Adrian Marin Mag, Paula Koelemeijer, Christophe Zaroli

Geophysical Journal International, 242(1), ggaf131

Read Online

This paper presents a unified mathematical framework for SOLA and DLI methods in seismic tomography, clarifying their connections and providing new insights for geophysical inverse problems.
